 "Hello World"

Hello! My name is Finlay Carson Moretti. Ever since I was young I was fascinated with the idea of making things with tech. Clay models of iPods, Paper Phones, Mii Characters and more.

Growing older, while others used their time to play football and play FPS games, I was building my first Augmented Reality experiences, printing 3D models, and making a brand for myself on YouTube. I was obsessed with finding ways to bring my creativity into the digital world.

Fast forward to today, I have mastered programs like Final Cut Pro, Blocs, Adobe XD and more, and I can’t wait to see where I take myself next.

How do you make your websites?

I use a development platform called Blocs. Blocs is built for designers, allowing me to easily translate my ideas for clients into functioning HTML, CSS and JS code.

Do you make money?

I only make money from paid projects with clients.
